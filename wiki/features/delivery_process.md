# Feature — Delivery Process (the playbook)

**Status:** draft v1 — 2026-05-29
**Goal:** a repeatable enquiry → delivered-site pipeline so every job is faster and more predictable than the last.

## The 5 stages

### 1. Enquiry
- Lead comes in via the landing page form, a call, or outreach reply.
- Capture: business name, current site URL (if any), what they do, what they want fixed.
- Auto-acknowledge fast. Speed of first response is a huge trust signal for SMBs.

### 2. Audit + quote (free, fast)
- Look at their current site: mobile, speed, clarity of contact/booking, design age.
- 10-minute screen-recording or one-page "here's what's wrong and what I'd do" — concrete, visual.
- Confirm the **flat fee** and what's included. One price, in writing.
- Goal: client says yes with zero anxiety about cost.

### 3. Gather (one short session)
- Collect: logo, photos, services list, opening hours, contact details, any reviews/testimonials.
- One structured intake form or one call — never make them chase or decide endlessly.
- Write the copy FOR them from this; don't ask a busy plumber to "send me your copy".

### 4. Build
- Standard site shape: Home, Services, About, Reviews, Contact/Book. Mobile-first.
- Use a repeatable template/system (stack TBD — see spec open question) so build time shrinks per job.
- One review round with the client. Set expectations: "one round of tweaks included."

### 5. Launch + handoff
- Point their domain, go live, check mobile + speed + forms work.
- Hand over simple "how to update X" notes or offer a light care plan (future upsell).
- Ask for a testimonial + referral while they're happy. This feeds outreach.

## Principles
- **Decisions made FOR them, not BY them.** Offer a default, let them veto.
- **Fixed scope.** One round of revisions. Extra = new quote. Protects margin and sanity.
- **Templatise relentlessly.** Every job should make the next one faster.

## Open questions
- Intake: form vs. call? (Form scales; call builds trust. Maybe form + optional call.)
- Where do client sites get hosted, and who pays for it?
- Care-plan upsell: offer at handoff or not at all in v1?
