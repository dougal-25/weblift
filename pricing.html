Pricing questions, answered

Is the price really fixed?

Yes. We quote one price upfront before any work starts. The only time it changes is if you later ask for something outside the original scope — and we'll quote that separately first.

Can I pay in stages?

On the Growth and Premium packages, yes — we can split it into stages so it's easier on cash flow.

What's included after launch?

One round of tweaks is included. After that we offer a low-cost care plan if you'd like us to keep it updated.

Do I own the website?

Yes — it's yours. We hand everything over at launch.

What's not included?

Hosting and your domain name are billed separately (they're cheap — we'll point you to the right options). Everything to design, write and build the site is in the package price.

Can I upgrade later?

Absolutely. Start with Basic and add pages or features down the line — we'll just quote the difference.

How do I pay?

A deposit to book your slot, the balance on launch. Growth and Premium can be split into stages.

Not sure which package fits?

Tell us about your business and we'll recommend the right one — free, no pressure.
